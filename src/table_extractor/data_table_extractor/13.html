Informatics Program Structure

Students need to have a background equivalent with a high school graduates majoring on science to be eligible for Informatics Engineering Degree program. Students with a background of vocational education informatics engineering or software engineering or computer science with good performance are also acceptable.

Curriculum of Informatics Undergraduate Program is divided into two stages: First Year Program and Scholar Program.
